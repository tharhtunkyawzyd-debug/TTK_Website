Bernese Oberland at Switzerland

  • The Iconic Trio: This region is home to the world-famous mountain trio—the Eiger, Mönch, and Jungfrau—standing side by side in a spectacular display of nature.
  • Top of Europe: Visit Jungfraujoch, Europe’s highest railway station, situated at 11,332 feet above sea level for breathtaking alpine views.
  • The Twin Lakes: Discover the world-renowned resort town of Interlaken, perfectly nestled between the crystal-clear turquoise waters of Lake Thun and Lake Brienz.
  • Cinematic Beauty: Experience the fairy-tale scenery of Iseltwald, the famous filming location of the piano scene from the popular K-drama "Crash Landing on You."
